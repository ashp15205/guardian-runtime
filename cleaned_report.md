Technical Comments

Review comments-1

1. Replace real and existing references with the fake references.
All the figures should be numbered, captioned and cited in the article content (for example,
“As shown in fig. 1, Figure 2 shows, etc.”).
Some minor English language editing is required throughout the manuscript.
2. Author should clarify the dataset preparation process for forgery detection training.
3. Provide more details about the proposed CNN model architecture.
4. Author should improve the explanation of the blockchain anchoring process.
5. Clarify how the system handles duplicate certificate detection.
6. Provide more comparison with recent certificate verification frameworks.
--
Review comments- 2

1.  The manuscript needs refinement in terms of technical clarity and content

organization.

2.  The objectives and scope of the study should be presented more explicitly.
3.  Some sections require additional supporting details and clearer explanations.
4.  The flow between introduction, methodology, and conclusion can be improved for

better coherence.

5.  The manuscript would benefit from thorough grammatical and typographical

corrections.

6.  Greater attention is needed in explaining the significance of the presented findings.
7.  References should be reviewed for relevance, consistency, and recent contributions.
8.  Certain figures and tables require better formatting and descriptive captions.
9.  The discussion lacks sufficient depth in connecting the results with existing studies.
10. The overall manuscript requires revision to enhance readability, consistency, and

technical presentation.
